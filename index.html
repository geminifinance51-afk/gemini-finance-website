About Us

Purpose: To empower PNG government employees with accessible and reliable financial solutions, fostering stability and confidence in meeting their short-term needs.

Goal: To provide 5,000 short-term personal loans to PNG government employees under the ALESCO Payroll System by the end of 2026, achieving a 95% customer satisfaction rate.

Mission Statement: Gemini Finance Limited delivers fast, reliable, and affordable short-term personal loans to PNG government employees under the ALESCO Payroll System, offering 24/7 service and loan approvals within 12 hours to bridge urgent cash gaps and support personal, family, and cultural obligations with trust and ease.

Vision Statement: To be Papua New Guinea’s leading provider of short-term financial solutions, offering unmatched 24/7 access and rapid loan approvals to empower government employees and drive financial resilience nationwide.

Gemini Finance Limited is committed to providing fast, flexible, and affordable short-term personal loans exclusively to PNG government employees under the ALESCO Payroll System. We help you manage urgent needs such as medical expenses, school fees, household bills, unexpected travel, and cultural obligations like bride price, funeral (haus krai) contributions, and compensation payments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply for a loan?

Only PNG government employees paid under the ALESCO Payroll System are eligible.

How much can I borrow?

Loan amounts range from K500 to K1,000, repayable over 3 to 10 fortnights.

What documents do I need?

Two latest payslips, employment confirmation letter, valid ID, completed loan application form, and Data Input Printout.

How fast will I get my loan?

Once approved, your loan is processed quickly and deducted automatically from your salary through ALESCO Payroll.

How It Works

1. Apply

Complete the loan application form and submit with all required documents.

2. Get Approved

We assess your application quickly and provide approval within 1–2 working days.

3. Receive Funds

Funds are released and repayments are deducted automatically through the ALESCO Payroll System.
