About Us

Mission: To deliver fast, reliable, and affordable financial solutions to PNG government employees, helping them bridge short-term cash gaps and meet urgent personal, family, and cultural obligations with confidence and ease.

Vision: To be the most trusted and preferred financial partner for PNG government employees, empowering them to achieve financial stability and fulfill cultural responsibilities with dignity and peace of mind.

Gemini Finance Limited is committed to providing fast, flexible, and affordable short-term personal loans exclusively to PNG government employees under the ALESCO Payroll System. We help you manage urgent needs such as medical expenses, school fees, household bills, unexpected travel, and cultural obligations like bride price, funeral (haus krai) contributions, and compensation payments.

Our Services

We provide short-term personal loans exclusively to PNG government employees under the ALESCO Payroll System. Loans range from K500 to K1,000, repayable over 3 to 10 fortnights, to help with:

  • Medical expenses
  • School fees
  • Household bills
  • Unexpected travel
  • Cultural obligations (e.g., bride price, funeral contributions, compensation payments)
